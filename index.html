White Rock Lake

White Rock Lake is a reservoir located in north-east Dallas. The lake was formed by damming White Rock Creek, which today widens into the lake before continuing south out of the spillway and emptying into the Trinity River. The lake covers 1,254 acres in the east Dallas community.
